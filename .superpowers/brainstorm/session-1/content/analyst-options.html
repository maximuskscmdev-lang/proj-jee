Phase 2: The Performance Analyst

Now that we have the simulator, let's build the brain of the app. How should we visualize your performance?

A

The Topic Heatmap

A color-coded grid (Red/Yellow/Green) of every sub-topic. Instantly see that you're weak in "Ionic Equilibrium" but strong in "Kinematics".

B

The Time-Bleed Detector

A chart showing exactly how many minutes you spent on each question vs. the average. Find those 10-minute "traps" that killed your score.

C

The Rank Predictor

A trend line showing your predicted AIR (All India Rank) based on your mock scores over time.
