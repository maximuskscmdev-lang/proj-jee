Map out your JEE Study Center

Since we're going with Focused Modules, let's refine the four main modes. Which one do you want to start with as our MVP (Minimum Viable Product)?

1

The Exam Simulator

A 1:1 replica of the JEE Advanced interface. Lag-free, high-fidelity, and distraction-free.

  • Official marking schemes
  • Mock paper generation
  • Time-pressure mode
2

The Concept Library

A fast, searchable repository of formulas, concept maps, and PYQs organized by topic.

  • Formula quick-sheets
  • PYQ search with tagging
  • Concept visualization
3

The Performance Analyst

Visual reports on your performance. It finds your weak sub-topics so you know what to fix.

  • Sub-topic accuracy heatmaps
  • Rank predictor engine
  • Trend visualization
4

The Smart Disciplinarian

A dynamic schedule builder that tells you exactly what to study now based on your performance data.

  • Automatic daily study tasks
  • Revision reminders
  • Adaptive rescheduling
